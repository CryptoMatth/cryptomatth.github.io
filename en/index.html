Welcome to CryptoMatth

What is CryptoMatth?

Welcome to CryptoMatth, a space dedicated to exploring and studying mathematics and cryptography. Born from a passion for these two fascinating fields, this site aims to gather, deepen, and share knowledge acquired over time.

In the Mathematics section, you'll find a comprehensive course covering the topics taught in the final three years of high school, enriched with an in-depth study of Calculus 1. Each topic is presented with detailed explanations and accompanied by practical exercises designed to reinforce theoretical concepts and enhance critical thinking.

The Cryptography section takes you through the history and evolution of ciphers, structured like a university-level course. Together, we'll explore encryption techniques, from classical methods to modern cryptographic systems, with clear examples and problem-solving exercises.

Whether you're a student seeking deeper insights, a mathematics enthusiast, or simply curious about the secrets of cryptography, CryptoMatth is the perfect place to fuel your curiosity and expand your knowledge.

Happy learning and happy browsing!
