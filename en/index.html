What is CryptoMatth?

Welcome to CryptoMatth, my space dedicated to the discovery and study of mathematics and cryptography. This site was born out of a passion for two fascinating worlds and aims to collect, deepen, and share the knowledge acquired over time.

In the Mathematics section, you will find a complete course covering the topics addressed in the last three years of high school, integrated with an in-depth study of Calculus 1. Each topic is presented with detailed explanations and accompanied by practical exercises, designed to consolidate theory and stimulate critical thinking.

The Cryptography section will guide you through the history and evolution of ciphers, treated like a real university course. Together, we will analyze the mechanisms of encryption systems, from classical methods to the most modern techniques, always accompanied by examples and problems to solve.

Whether you are a student looking for in-depth studies, a mathematics enthusiast, or simply curious to learn the secrets of cryptography, CryptoMatth is the ideal place to stimulate your curiosity and expand your skills.

Happy studying and happy browsing!
